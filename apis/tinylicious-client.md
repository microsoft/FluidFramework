The \*\*tinylicious-client\*\* package provides a simple and powerful way to consume collaborative Fluid data with the Tinylicious service.

The Tinylicious service is a local, in-memory Fluid service intended for prototyping and development purposes.

See [https://fluidframework.com/docs/testing/tinylicious/](https://fluidframework.com/docs/testing/tinylicious/)
