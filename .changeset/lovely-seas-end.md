-   `GCDataBuilder`
-   `getGCDataFromSnapshot`
-   `IGCResult`
-   `removeRouteFromAllNodes`
-   `runGarbageCollection`
-   `trimLeadingAndTrailingSlashes`
-   `trimLeadingSlashes`
-   `trimTrailingSlashes`
-   `unpackChildNodesGCDetails`
-   `unpackChildNodesUsedRoutes`
