[Packages](/docs/apis/) &gt; [@fluid-experimental/devtools](/docs/apis/devtools)

Primary entry-point to the Fluid Devtools.

To initialize the Devtools alongside your application's [IFluidContainer](/docs/apis/fluid-static/ifluidcontainer-interface), call [initializeDevtools](/docs/apis/devtools#initializedevtools-function).

The Devtools will automatically dispose of themselves upon Window unload, but if you would like to close them earlier, call _IDevtools.dispose_.

To enable visualization of Telemetry data, you may create a [DevtoolsLogger](/docs/apis/devtools-core/devtoolslogger-class) and provide it during Devtools initialization.
